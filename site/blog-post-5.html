Tree Hut Moroccan Rose Shea Sugar Scrub

by Daisy Aug 14, 2023

The best $10 you’ll ever spend at Ulta, hands down!

And I’m not just saying that because a tub of Tree Hut Moroccan Rose Shea Sugar Scrub brought me back to life after three days camping in the hot, dusty woods.

But after watching a ribbon of dirt dance down the drain after exfoliating my body with this sugary, rose scented scrub, I couldn’t help but think, “Wow, that’s the most beautifully disgusting, yet satisfying, thing I’ve seen in a really long time.”

The level of physical exfoliation in this product does not play! It’s intense. However, it doesn’t hurt, which is a big deal for me. I don’t want to be punished by my beauty products, ya know? Life is hard enough.

Sugar and silica (here’s some in-depth ingredient info on silica) lift away all the stuff sitting on your skin to reveal softer, smoother EVERYTHING.

Oh, and it fills your bathroom with the scent of a big, blooming bouquet of lush, fresh roses. Lovely!

Last but not least, you don’t have to worry about the product leaving an oily film behind. The scrub boasts a “no-slip” formula, so feel free to move about your tub without worrying about falling to your unexpected doom.

By the way, at Ulta, it’s currently 10% off when you buy online and pick up in store with code SUMMER23.

Soft skin for everyone!
